Get involved

We believe that regardless of one's reasons to get involved in the realization of someone else's dream, one should have fun! That will take one's involvement to another level - turning it into a creative, inspiring and fullfilling experience for all involved.

Since one's involvement is up to every individual, there's no shortage of ways to get involved. One may choose to, among other things:

  • Spread the word.
  • Organize an event to raise funds or awareness for the year's project.
  • Host events organized by others.
  • Support the project by participating in a paid event.
  • Sponsor the project.
  • Help with know-how, time, resources, tips or suggestions.

There's something very special in getting involved in the realization of someone else's dream, but don't take our word for it. Do get involved and find out for yourself!
