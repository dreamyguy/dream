Help someone realize their dream

Dream-it-forward is an initiative to help people realize their most cherished dream. It could be a goal that has proven too challenging or impossible for a single person to achieve, or simply down-prioritized for too long by "life-as-it-happens".

Surprisingly, helping someone else realize their dream is much easier than it seems at first. All they need is a little help, a tiny spark to get the fire going. And then it suddenly goes WHOOSH!

Get involved

We believe that regardless of one's reasons to get involved in the realization of someone else's dream, one should have fun! That will take one's involvement to another level - turning it into a creative, inspiring and fullfilling experience for all involved.

Since one's involvement is up to every individual, there's no shortage of ways to get involved.

There's something very special in getting involved in the realization of someone else's dream, but don't take our word for it. Do get involved and find out for yourself!

  • Spread the word!
  • Organize an event to raise funds or awareness for the year's project.
  • Host events organized by others.
  • Support the project by participating in a paid event.
  • Sponsor the project.
  • Help with know-how, time, resources, tips or suggestions.

Help us spread the word!

Every single share helps! We have no marketing budget and rely solely on word-of-mouth. Share this page on your social networks and let everyone know they can help someone realize their dream - in whichever way they'd like to do so.
